GIS & Remote Sensing Portfolio • East Carolina University

I analyze urban hydrology and land-use change using ArcGIS Pro, Python, and remote-sensing workflows. Recent work includes hydrologically conditioned DEMs, nutrient export modeling (InVEST NDR), urban stormwater retention (USR), flood-risk mitigation (UFRM), and Landsat-based land cover change for Greenville, NC and the Savannah, GA region.
Tools: ArcGIS Pro · ArcPy · InVEST (NDR/USR/UFRM) · Landsat/NAIP · Raster analysis · Spatial statistics
