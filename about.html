About GeoSite Intel

GeoSite Intel provides pre-construction drainage and terrain risk screening for builders and development teams.

Before grading begins and before permits are submitted, terrain already contains signals. Low-elevation pockets, runoff pathways, slope transitions, and road-to-flow conflicts can indicate where drainage issues may show up later. GeoSite Intel identifies those signals early and turns them into clear, decision-ready screening maps.

  • ▸Drainage & terrain screening
  • ▸Runoff pathways & low-spot cues
  • ▸LiDAR DEM analysis
  • ▸Road / drainage conflict flags
  • ▸Clear layouts for fast review
  • ▸Optional GIS + mapping support

Note: Deliverables are for screening, communication, and prioritization — not sealed engineering design.

What this helps prevent

Surprises cost money. Screening early helps reduce rework, delays, grading revisions, and water-related complaints by flagging likely problem zones before a project is too far along to pivot easily.
