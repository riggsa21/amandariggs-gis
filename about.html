About

GeoSite Intel — a freelance geospatial analyst focused on site intelligence: turning terrain, drainage, and environmental signals into maps that are clean, practical, and decision-ready.

My work sits at the intersection of geospatial, environmental, and engineering-style screening. That means repeatable workflows (LiDAR → terrain → flow cues → risk flags) and deliverables that stakeholders can understand fast.

  • ▸Drainage & terrain screening
  • ▸Hydrology / watershed mapping
  • ▸LiDAR DEM processing
  • ▸Land-use change & remote sensing
  • ▸Spatial modeling & QA-ready layouts
  • ▸Python/ArcPy automation

Note: My outputs are designed for planning, screening, communication, and prioritization. They are not a substitute for sealed engineering design documents.

Background

I hold a B.S. in Environmental Studies, an M.S. in Geography, and a GIS Certificate. I’m especially interested in hydrology, land-use change, and how urban development shifts runoff, flood risk, and ecosystem services.

If you’re deciding between lots, troubleshooting a drainage issue, or preparing for stakeholder conversations, I can help you turn the data into a map that actually answers the question.
